Our Story

More Than a Store —
We're Family

Founded in 2009, Nanak Pet Store was born out of a deep passion for animals and the belief that every pet deserves exceptional care.

A Legacy Built on Love for Animals

Nanak Pet Store was founded by Gurpreet Nanak, a lifelong animal lover who saw a gap in the market for a truly personal, community-driven pet shop. Starting with a small storefront and a big heart, the store has grown into one of the most trusted pet destinations in the city.

Over 15 years, we've welcomed thousands of families through our doors — helping first-time pet owners, supporting rescue animals, and celebrating milestones with the loyal pets we've all come to know by name.

We believe pets are not just animals — they're family. That philosophy drives every product we stock and every service we offer.

Compassion

We care deeply about every animal that comes through our doors.

Quality

Only the best, vet-recommended products make our shelves.

Community

We support local shelters and host community pet events regularly.

Sustainability

We prioritize eco-friendly, ethically sourced pet products.
